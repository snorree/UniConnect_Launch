Privacy Policy for Uniconnect

Effective Date: May 09, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to UniConnect. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit and interact with our landing page.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information from two types of users: Prospective Students and Current University Students.

For Prospective Students:

When you sign up on our landing page, we collect the following details:

  • Name
  • Current academic year
  • Email address
  • Universities of interest
  • Services of interest, including:
    • General Consultation
    • Interview Help
    • Personal Statement Guidance
    • Entrance Exam Tips
    • Life at University
    • Academic Experience
    • Any additional interests you specify
For Current University Students:

To participate in consultations, we collect:

  • Name
  • University name
  • University email address (e.g., ending in .ac.uk or .edu)
  • Course of study
  • Topics you are comfortable discussing about university life
How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate connections between prospective students and current university students.
  • To personalize the user experience based on interests and preferences.
  • To communicate relevant updates, service offerings, and opportunities.
  • To verify the authenticity of university student participants.
Data Protection and Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Your data is stored securely, and we do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to process data, such as email communication platforms. These services comply with industry security standards and are used solely to support our platform's functions.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our landing page may use cookies to enhance user experience and track site usage. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Opt out of communications at any time by contacting us.
Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at hello@uniconnect.global
