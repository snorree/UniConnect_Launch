How UniConnect Works

1

Register Your Interest

Whether you're a prospective student seeking guidance or a university student willing to share your experience, start by registering on our platform. We'll collect some basic information to understand your needs or expertise.

2

Get Matched

We'll match prospective students with current university students based on their university preferences, course interests, and specific areas of guidance needed. This ensures meaningful and relevant connections.

3

Schedule Your Consultation

Once matched, you'll receive access to schedule a video consultation at a time that works for both parties. Sessions typically last 30-45 minutes, giving you plenty of time to discuss your questions and concerns.

4

Have Your Consultation

Connect with your matched student via our secure video platform. Get honest insights about university life, course experiences, and valuable tips for your application process. For university students, share your knowledge while earning.

For Prospective Students

Get authentic insights from students who've been in your shoes. Discuss everything from application strategies and interview preparation to campus life and course experiences. Our consultations are designed to give you the real picture of university life.

For University Students

Share your valuable experience while earning money. Help prospective students make informed decisions about their university journey. Your insights can make a real difference in someone's educational path.
